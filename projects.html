My Malls

MyMalls is an international shipping solution designed to be easy and hassle-free. It allows users worldwide to shop from any online store, offering live support and the option to combine multiple packages into one to save on shipping costs. It works by providing a U.S. address where users can send their tax-free purchases, and MyMalls then delivers them to your home or business.

Built with: Swift / UIKit / Xcode / AppCenter / Firebase / MVVM Architecture / Mixpanel / Sentry / DeepLink / Push Notifications
